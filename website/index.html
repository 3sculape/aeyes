February 2021

This marks the very beginning of the project Forsaken and at the same time of the studio XII Eyes. For now Forsaken is just an idea and some sketches on paper. But the ambition is big and the motivation unfailing. The visual identity of the project is created and the project ready to start!

March 2021

After two months of fine-tuning the gameplay ideas and concepts as well as the art direction, the project gradually becomes concrete. The foundations of the procedural generation algorithm are established and the first designs and 3D models are created.

May 2021

One month later, the multiplayer system is implemented and functional. The player and enemies models are rigged-up and ready to be animated. Forsaken has a musical theme and sound effects.

June 2021

Finally, 5 months after the start of the project, Forsaken is officially finished and available to the world!
